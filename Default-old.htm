Who is Yuandan Lin

  • Assistant Professor, Department of Mathematical Sciences, Florida Atlantic University, since August 1992.
  • Ph.D. in Mathematics, Department of Mathematics, Rutgers University, 1992.
    Thesis advisor: Professor Eduardo D. Sontag.
    Thesis title: Lyapunov Function Techniques for Stabilization

Yuandan Lin's Research Interests

Dr. Lin's current research interests lie in nonlinear control theory, with emphasis on the Lyapunov theory of stability and stabilizability of general nonlinear systems.
